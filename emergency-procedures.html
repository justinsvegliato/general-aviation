If landing at non-towered airport:
  • Land using standard traffic pattern procedures.
  • Call Oakland Flyers at (510) 568-3317.
  • Call Oakland Tower for return instructions.
If landing at towered airport:
  • Remain outside Class D airspace.
  • Determine traffic flow.
  • Enter on 45 aiming at tower.
  • Watch for light signals.
  • Respond with light flashes or wing wags.
